Tribute to Eliot Lara

  • 1972: My dad was born in Guatemala City, Guatemala. The youngest of over a dozen children.
  • 1984: At the age of 12, he immigrated to the United States with his mom, cousin, and a bicycle.
  • 1991: He completed middle school and attended James Monroe High School, graduating in the 1990s
  • 1990s: He met his first wife, Catalina Alvarez, through a mutual Latina friend.
  • 2000: After their marriage, they had their first child, Matthew Adam Lara
  • 2002: Two years later they had their second, Andrew Lara
  • 2000-2024: For two decades, Eliot supported his family through all financial hardship
  • May 2024: Eliot got to watch his youngest son graduate college.
